Muon Mass Experiment

My senior thesis at Reed College focused on developing a novel experimental approach to measuring the mass of cosmic-ray muons. After graduation, we continued researching and refining the method. The results have since been published in the American Journal of Physics (Vol. 85, Is. 8).
